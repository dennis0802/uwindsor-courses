Website with Good Design

Websites are designed well based on these basic elements of web design: Content, usability, aesthethics, visibility, and interaction
(from 5 Basic Elements of Web Design). I believe this website has good design based on these principles which I can explain using these elements:

Content

The content of a website should be relevant to what it's about. This allows the website to engage with users
and stay on it longer. For anyone interested in news for the local Windsor/Metro Detroit areas and information from the AM800
radio station, the articles and content engages with the user.

Usability

An unusuable website will stick out. Websites should be easy to navigate, intuitive, and accessible to all users,
no matter their device and network. The news articles can be navigated to one another and it's intuitive to navigate
the website. In addition, the website is usable when on a mobile device. Articles can be found in the centre of the page
with navigation at the very top and some side information as an aside.

Aesthetics

Websites should be visually appealing, especially for businesses who must reflect their brand image. Websites should
be able to visually connect with your audience. The website's colours don't distract the user from being engaged and
complement the radio station's brand image.

Visibility

Having your website being easily found is important. Using SEO (search engine optimization) is important to good web design.
Being a local news radio station, it can easily be found by residents of the region and anyone tuning in while driving.
When searching up the name on Google, the website is the 1st one to come up, showing good SEO.

Interaction

Your website should engage with your audience and encourage them to contact you. The articles and the option to listen from
the website allows it to engage with the audience. At the footer is contact info for the radio station, allowing end-users
to contact the radio station.

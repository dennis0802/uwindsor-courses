My Winter 2021 Schedule

Here is my schedule for the Winter 2021 semester at the University of Windsor, each class joined with an image that
I feel best represents it. Because of the current pandemic since March 2020, all classes and exams have been held online.
In the past, I have taken mostly math and computer science courses for my degree. I was taking a decent amount of distance
education courses when the pandemic began so I was used to online learning for some of classes from the start. However, some classes
I've had to get used to being online because of the nature of the class, such as my math classes. Overall, the online learning format
can work if everything is implemented correctly and the curriculum can still be conveyed through a screen and not in real
time. In some cases, it might not work simply due to a practical/application nature of the class.
